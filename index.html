UDSM Expands to Lindi: New Campus Opens in Southern Tanzania

Prime Minister Dr. Mwigulu Lameck Nchemba has described the establishment of UDSM Lindi Campus as a transformative initiative for agriculture-led development in Southern Tanzania. Speaking at the foundation stone laying ceremony at Ngongo on 20th December 2025, Dr. Nchemba emphasized that the campus will provide modern knowledge, research, and innovation to benefit farmers and rural communities. The campus, implemented under the HEET Project, will initially focus on agriculture-related programmes, agri-business, and food processing technologies, with future expansion into oil and gas studies to meet the region's energy sector needs.
